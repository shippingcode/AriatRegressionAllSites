*************************** Create account UK test ******************************************

Create account test1: create Account
1. Access environment ariat development
2. Choose UK
3. Go to Sign In page
4. Go to Create account
5. Enter first name, last name, birth month, birth day, check gender, fill email, confirmation email, passwd and conf passwd (valid info)
6. Check add me to Ariat email list
7. Create account (My account page is displayed)
8. Logout from My account

Create account test3: returning Customer
1. Access environment ariat development
2. Choose UK
3. Go to Sign In page

5. Press Login button (My account page is displayed)
6. Logout from My account
