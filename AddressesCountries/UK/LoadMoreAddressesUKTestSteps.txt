**********************Load more addresses UK test steps***********************************************************************

Load more addresses test1
1. Go to development.ariat.com
2. Close subscription
3. Choose UK as location
4. Go to Sign in page
5. Enter username & password
6. Press "login" button
7. Go to "Edit" link in middle navigation - My account
8. Press "Load more" so all the addresses are displayed until load more button is no longer available
9. Go to My account in top right navigation
10. Click logout


Load more addresses test2
1. Go to development.ariat.com
2. Close subscription
3. Choose UK as location
4. Go to Sign in page
5. Enter username & password
6. Press "login" button
7. Click "Addresses" link in left navigation- My account
8. Press "Load more" so all the addresses are displayed until load more button is no longer available
9. Go to My account in top right navigation
10. Click logout
